Rhode Island SSBU Homepage

Welcome to the Official Rhode Island Super Smash Bros Ultimate Website!

What is the Rhode Island Super Smash Bros Scene?

We are a grassroots esports community from Rhode Island who host events for Super Smash Bros Ultimate! We host tournaments for gamers to test their skills and compete throughout the state, as well as publish rankings of players at the end of each three month competitive season. We are part of the larger New England Super Smash Bros community.

What is this site?

This site will act as a hub of easy to access information about our scene, including an event calendar, weekly and monthly event pages, a comprehensive ranking information page, links to the various social media pages, and tools for Tournament Organizers to promote their events.
