URI Rhody Rumble Weekly

The University of Rhode Island Gaming Club's premier weekly Super Smash Bros and Rivals of Aether 2 tournament!

  • Held in the URI Memorial Union, 50 Lower College Rd, Kingston, RI, 02881, USA
  • Room 312A / 360
  • Every Friday Night
    • Ultimate Singles Starts at 6 PM
    • Rivals of Aether 2 Starts at 6:30 PM

URI Rhody Ruckus Monthly

The University of Rhode Island Gaming Club's (slighly inconsistent) monthly event, featuring Super Smash Bros Ultimate singles and doubles, Rivals of Aether 2, as well as other fighting game side brackets such as Street Fighter 6, Tekken 8, Guilty Gear: Strive, and more!

  • Held in the URI Memorial Union, 50 Lower College Rd, Kingston, RI, 02881, USA
  • Atrium 1/2 or Rainville Ballroom
  • Once a Month Large Scale Events
    • Smash Ultimate Doubles at 12 PM
    • Rivals of Aether 2 Singles at 2 PM
    • Ultimate Singles at 4pm
    • Rivals of Aether 2 Doubles at 5pm
    • Fighting Game Times to be Announced
  • 96 Entrant Cap
