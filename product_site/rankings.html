What is a Power Ranking?

A Power Ranking is a list of the best players in any given region, in this case, all of Rhode Island. Rhode Island Power Rankings release quarterly, ranging from 5 to 10 players, with varying requirements based on the season, event frequency, and player activity. The Rhode Island PR Panel is a group of Tournament Organizers and Community Figureheads who compile data about each player who meets the requirements for any given season, and chooses the order based off of these factors:

  • Placements: results at tournaments
  • Head to Heads: head to head set records VS current Rhode Island ranked players and PR contenders
  • Wins: bracket sets won at eligible tournaments
  • Losses: bracket sets lost at eligible tournaments

Current RI PR Requirements

These requirements are set by the RI PR Panel: DYL, Paulo and MAMMOTH

  • Attendence: At least 2 Rhode Island Brackets, 4 Brackets total
  • Tournament Eligibility: Has a prize (cash or equivalent), and at least 12 entrants
  • Residency: Must be living in Rhode Island for more than half of the season
