My Favorite Dish: Sauteed Pierogies

This is a traditional polish dish and a staple in my household. It's a simple recepie, with pierogies, which are traditional polish dumplings, sauteed in a frying pan with onions, garlic, salt and pepper for flavor. The pierogies can be homeade with any additional fillings, alongside the standard potato and cheese, or simply storebought for convinience. I learned the recipe from my dad.

Mithilesh's Favorite Dish: Papdi Chaat

Papdi chaat is crispy crackers topped with multiple ingredients usually with boiled potatoes, onions, chickpeas, tomatoes, curd, sev, and chutneys.

Gordon's Favorite Dish: Homeade Tacos

Homemade tacos that include ground beef. Classic family style dinner with hard or soft shells. Includes a variety of Mexican style ingredients.
